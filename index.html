About ZKBOY

The ZBOY project aims to introduce individuals to the zkSync ecosystem through its great and energetic mascot, ZKBOY! The project is community-owned and managed, with strict limits on individual holdings to promote decentralization and widespread adoption. By leveraging the power of memes and community-driven content creation, ZKBOY aims to increase awareness and understanding of the zkSync universe, serving as a guide and companion to all who embark on this journey!

Secure & Safe

The $ZKBOY smart contract has been fully audited by a top specialized team. The ownership of the contract has been renounced and the Liquidity Pool tokens have been locked for a year.That means next to zero risk to our users and community from potential bad actors.

Community

From the start, ZKBOY was designed to support a large number of smaller token holders, who will all participate in and benefit from the growth and development of the ZKBOY Community. All projects and initiatives will be community-led and will result in additional value being brought into the ZKBOY and zkSync ecosystems. Everyone is welcome to join the community and help it grow!

Token Economy Model

:1. Deflationary mechanism: ZKBOY adopts a deflationary design with a limited total token supply and no additional issuance. A portion of each transaction will be burned, gradually reducing the token supply and increasing the value of the token. 2. Transaction tax: Each ZKBOY transaction will be subject to a 4% tax, which will be allocated as follows: 1% Burn: Reducing token supply to achieve a deflationary effect. 1% Marketing and community rewards: Used for promoting the project, hosting events, and rewarding community members for their participation. 2% Automatic liquidity pool: Used to improve the liquidity and stability of the token.!
